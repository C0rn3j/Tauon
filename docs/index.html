A modern and streamlined player that puts you in control of your music library

  • Easy drag and drop track importing
  • Assumes folders are albums
  • Functions for renaming and deleting folders
  • Function for renaming tracks in folders
  • Open tracks in your favourite tag editor
  • No hidden changes to your music library in the background

Playlists at the forefront

  • Quickly create, filter, combine and delete playlists
  • No need to create a 'library' to get started
  • Export and import playlists in the XSPF file format

Visualise your playtimes

  • Visually compare individual track play times
  • Keep play counts when moving your library
  • Scrobble and love tracks on Last.fm

Inbound and outbound streaming

  • Broadcast asynchronously like a DJ How?
  • Let listeners check out album art and lyrics
  • Automatic splitting when recording inbound steams

Shrink your library for your PMP (requires ffmpeg)

  • Simple batch transcoding of albums to single folder How?
  • Select between OGG and OPUS
  • Automatically includes album art thumbnail

And more!

  • Supports codecs MP3, FLAC plus others
  • Automatic .zip extracting
  • Automatic .cue handling
  • Streamlined quick search
  • Large album art display
  • Integrated gallery view
